## The problem

Every team built its own dashboard pipeline: nightly SQL dumps, ad-hoc
Python, a wall of stale Grafana boards. Product decisions waited a day
for data that already existed.

## Constraints

- The warehouse budget was fixed — "faster" could not mean "bigger bill".
- Analysts wrote SQL, not Scala: the pipeline had to end in plain tables.

## The approach

One ingestion path (Kafka), one transformation layer (Spark streaming
jobs compiled from declarative configs), one serving store (ClickHouse).
Delete every bespoke pipeline the week its team migrated.

```sql
-- The whole contract for a new dashboard: one materialized view.
CREATE MATERIALIZED VIEW mv_orders_by_region
ENGINE = SummingMergeTree
ORDER BY (region, toStartOfHour(created_at))
AS SELECT region, toStartOfHour(created_at) AS h, count() AS orders
FROM events.orders GROUP BY region, h;
```

## Outcome

- 80M events/day, dashboards fresh within 90 seconds.
- Infra cost cut in half — ClickHouse replaced three warehouse workloads.
- 200+ dashboards migrated; the old pipelines were deleted, not archived.
